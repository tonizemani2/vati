# /gate — is it priced in? (the test that defines the edge)

A low-saturation needle is only a *candidate*. The edge exists **only in the gap** between where the constraint is going and where consensus already sits. This runs that test — broadly, not just on a stock — and writes the reasoning down. **You decide; nothing is promoted without your nod.** Read `doctrine.md` (Pillar 7 = the priced-in benchmark). $0/keyless; log before any spend.

## Priced-in is MULTI-CHANNEL — never finance-only
"Is it priced in?" is not "is there a stock trade." Read every channel that shows the crowd has arrived; a market price is the *sharpest* one **only where a clean instrument exists**:
- **Narrative** — `saturation-topic "<claim>"`. Trade press / analyst coverage / popular books + macro newsletters.
- **Forecaster (the structural-altitude channel)** — `consensus-eye "<the structural claim>"`: have forecasters / specialist press **already projected** the reorganization? **HONEST ASYMMETRY (locked):** the eye reliably certifies **PRICED**, it can **never** certify pre-consensus — keyless search is blind to paywalled sell-side notes + specialist B2B trade press, so `UNCONFIRMED` means *"I couldn't see it, YOU judge,"* never a green light. A thesis good enough to be mechanistically sound in a hot domain has usually already been written by a sell-side analyst → **assume covered until you've checked the specialist layer.** Query a tight topic phrase, not the full claim sentence (the sentence searches poorly).
- **Recursive layer-blindness** — when the obvious deep layer already has a published thesis (enrichment, jet engines), the edge is the layer **beneath that** (uranium *conversion*/UF₆ reads narrative 0.04 vs enrichment 0.65; hot-section single-crystal castings under engines). Keep going down until you hit a layer with no published thesis — or one too small to matter (the honest stop).
- **Capital** — do the LAG channels fire (SEC filings, capex, VC/M&A)? `discover` shows lead-vs-lag per entity. Capital arriving = priced. **Acquisition of the pure-play** (e.g. Capstan → AbbVie) = smart money already in = priced.
- **Policy** — Federal Register / export-controls / procurement already acting = priced.
- **Price** — ONLY if a clean inelastic-vs-elastic public pair exists: `discover-instruments` → `consensus-score`. A diversified parent (TMO) or a foreign listing (OXB.L) does **not** give a clean read — say so, don't fake one.

Verdict = **PRE-CONSENSUS** (all channels quiet) · **PARTLY-PRICED** (one fired) · **PRICED** (forecasters/price/capital here). Edge lives only in the first.

**Then check the kind, not just the channels.** Run `base-rates`: a call tagged `hype_overpriced` or `horizon_gap` has ~0% measured hit-rate in our own §8 record — a quiet narrative does NOT rescue it. Long-dated + welded to a hot narrative is the trap; short-fused single-mechanism (`layer_blindness` / `cost_curve_breakout`, ~100%) is the harvestable kind.

## Physical-primary, financial-optional (locked rule)
The forecast is on the **constraint metric** — the input's capacity / lead-time / output / its own price series — NOT a stock P&L. Express the thesis physically; a financial pair is an *optional* second expression, never the proof. A needle with no clean instrument is still valid — judge it on physical + narrative + policy, and **say plainly there's no equity play**.

## Procedure
1. **Log the survivors** as gated Hypotheses (`hypothesis-add`, every disciplinary field). Persists the *why* in the cockpit; reversible; $0.
2. **Run the multi-channel priced-in read** on each. State the verdict per channel + overall, with cited evidence.
3. **Name the physical falsifier** for each surviving PRE-CONSENSUS needle: the dated, numeric constraint-metric threshold + kill-criteria (what a ForecastCard would score).
4. **Stop.** Hand back the verdicts + the physical falsifier. Promote NOTHING.

## Output contract
Per needle, ≤7 lines: priced-in verdict (per-channel: narrative / capital / policy / price-or-"no clean instrument") · the GAP (consensus now vs where the constraint is going) · the physical falsifier + horizon · optional equity pair (or "none clean") · logged hypothesis id.
Then one line: which needle has the widest un-priced gap, and why.

## Hard rules
- **Never** call something priced/un-priced on the stock channel alone. No clean instrument ≠ no edge.
- **Never** promote to a ForecastCard or place a bet — Ruben's call (rule 7, ask-don't-assume). >$0 needs approval.
- If a needle turns out PRICED, say so plainly and drop it — a killed candidate is the gate working, not a failure.
